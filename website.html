Welcome to My College Website

Welcome to STMWEC College of Engineering Guntur,AP.

St. Mary’s Women’s Engineering College was established in the year of 2008. The college is popularly known as SMWEC in Guntur. The institute offers a wide array of academic courses and programmes that are approved by AICTE.The college, affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada(JNTUK) , is widely recognized for its academic standards. St. Mary’s Womens Engineering College (SMWEC), Guntur takes a holistic view of education and focuses on both, academic as well as personality development of the students.

My 5 Favorite Things About STMWEC

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is an academic program that integrates the fields of computer engineering and computer science. CSE also include core subjects of computer science such as theory of computation, design and analysis of algorithms, data structures and database systems. in this computer science engineering.

  1. B.TECH(AI)
  2. B.TECH(ML)
  3. B.TECH(AI&DS)
  4. B.TECH(AI&ML)
  5. B.TECH(DS)
